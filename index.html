About the school

We offer a high school curriculum of the highest standards for our students. Through it we strive to develop the intellectual, social, and personal aspects of our students' lives. We are able to achieve this using the very best learning resources at our disposal, together with our experienced educators and an unconventional learning environment. We have developed a personalised learning model, with a focus on learning experiences beyond just the classroom. Our traditional teaching methods are supplemented by smart education technologies, which we believe are a crucial element to modern education methods. Our multi-disciplinary approach allows our students to breach the barriers of a blinkered approach to life with an open mind, enthusiasm, a desire to solve problems and take calculated risks in an ever more competitive world.
